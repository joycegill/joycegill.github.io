About Me

I'm a junior at Grinnell College, majoring in Computer Science with concentrations in Statistics and Digital Studies. At Grinnell College, I am working as an AI Research Assistant at Professor Weinman's lab, Course Mentor for CSC 208: Discrete Structures, Lead Instructional Coordinator at the Grinnell Middle School, Admissions Tour Guide, and Information Session Co-Host.

This summer, I will be joining Carnegie Mellon University's Human-Computer Interaction Institute as a part of their Summer 2025 Research Program (REU). I will be working as an undergraduate researcher at Professor Paulo Carvalho's Optimized Algorithms and Knowledge (OAK) Lab.

Last summer, I worked at Columbia University as a part of the Amazon SURE Research Program. I served as an undergraduate human-computer interaction researcher at Professor Brian Smith's Computer-Enabled Abilities Lab (CEAL).
